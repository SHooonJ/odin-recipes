ColdBrew Latte

Description

A simple coffee bevereage. Great for days where time is tight!

Ingredients

  • 4 floz coldbrew concentrate
  • 6 floz milk (whole, almond, oat)
  • 20 grams of simple syrup

Steps

  1. Pour 4 floz of coldbrew into the cup
  2. Pour 6 floz of milk into the cup
  3. Add 20 grams of simple syrup
